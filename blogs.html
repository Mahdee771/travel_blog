We arrived around 12.00 AM. We stayed in a camping site on the way. It was magical there. The best part was there was no one except us. The night, the stars, the nature it was more than we expected. We had a view of river and mountain just in front of our place. We stargaed all night. Had snacks,drinks,songs. We even explored the resort. That was once in a lifetime experience for me. We feared about the ghosts, prayed our salah, wondered at night and what not? Can't complain about anything. Subhanallah!!

In the early morning, after the campsite night, we started our journey towards our actual desination. The Kaptai Lake. At first we had our breakfast in a nearby hotel. Then we book a boat for one hour at 400 taka. We went to a place to have swimming. We spent about 3 hours there. On the way back, We had our lunch in the same hotel. After that we went to a resort called Panorama Zoom Resort. Which is managed by the BGB. We hired kayak for 200 tk an hour per kayak. Which can accomodate 2 person.
